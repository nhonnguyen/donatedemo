1

Download Wallet

Download the Trust Wallet app or Metamask

2

Buy BNB

Buy BNB from any exchange and transfer it into your Wallet.

3

Add $DNT to your wallet

Go to PancakeSwap in your internet browser and connect your wallet by clicking "Connect Wallet" and select WalletConnect. Then connect to your Metamask Wallet account.

4

Open Pancakeswap

Go to PancakeSwap in your internet browser and connect your wallet by clicking "Connect Wallet" and select WalletConnect. Then connect to your Metamask Wallet account.

5

Add $DNT on Pancakeswap

On PancakeSwap, select "Add a currency" and paste the $HELP contract address. Choose the amount of BNB you would like to swap to DNT and set the slippage tolerance to 12% in the Settings.

6

BUY $DNT

Hit the "Swap" button. Congratulations! You now have your very own DNT tokens and are supporting the mission to rebuild the country is in state of war.
